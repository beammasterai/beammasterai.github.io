About BeamMaster AI

BeamMaster AI is a state-of-the-art, open-source civil engineering platform dedicated to providing student architects, civil engineering undergraduates, teachers, and structural designers with highly accessible, beautiful, and computationally precise tools.

The Core Philosophy

Conventional structural software packages are either overly complex (like large FEM packages) or look outdated and hard to navigate. BeamMaster AI merges **beautiful responsive styling** with **analytical structural calculations**, allowing engineers to rapidly prototype calculations and students to visualize instantly how beam equations translate into shapes.

Technical Engine

Calculations in BeamMaster AI are solved analytically. By using closed-form solutions (like mathematical superposition matrices for support forces and direct double integration algorithms on bending moments), we ensure zero rounding error, accurate locations of shear changes, and smooth curvature calculations for deflection curves.
